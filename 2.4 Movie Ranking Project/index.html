The Best Movies According to Clare

My top 3 movies of all-time

Harry Potter 1-7

These films are timeless and magical. They take you on the journey of a young boy called Harry Potter, who learns that he is a wizard and a famous one too.

Star Wars - Episodes IV, V and VI

Out of all the star wars movies these are still by far my favourite. The effects they created back in the 70's/80's were unbelievable and some of the best cinematography.

Labyrinth

A musical fantasy featuring the amazing David Bowie as the the Goblin King. I loved the characters in this movie.
